🚗 About Safe Raahi

Safe Raahi is an innovative, real-time road safety system developed to protect drivers during foggy or low-visibility conditions. By combining GPS, AI-driven driver monitoring, Firebase integration, and SOS communication, it creates a safer environment for every journey. Whether you’re navigating highways at night or driving through dense fog, Safe Raahi is your co-pilot for smart, secure travel. 🛣️

🔧 Key Features

  • 🚨 Vehicle Proximity Alerts: Detects nearby vehicles within 1 km using real-time GPS, minimizing collision risk in poor visibility.
  • 📍 Live Location Tracking: Monitors your position live with Firebase support, ideal for emergency tracking and navigation.
  • 🆘 Emergency SOS Support: Tap-to-call for help, auto-sends live location via internet or SMS fallback during no-network zones.
  • ⚡ EV Charging Station Finder: Discover nearby charging stations with live availability and get instant directions.
  • ⛽ Petrol Pump Locator: Easily find the closest fuel stations on highways and expressways with real-time distance updates.
  • 😴 Drowsy Driver Alert: Uses AI and front-camera to detect eye closure. Loud alerts wake you up when you need it most.
  • 🎛️ Customizable Settings: Personalize your experience with toggles for dark mode, sound, and location updates.
  • 📘 Built-in Help Guide: Step-by-step guidance within the app to help you use features with ease and confidence.
